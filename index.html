Frequently Asked Questions

Is it legal to share streaming subscriptions?

Most streaming services allow account sharing within a household or family. Our platform helps you connect with others in a way that respects each service's terms of use. We provide clear guidelines for each service to ensure compliance.

How do payments work?

Our secure payment system handles all transactions between members. The subscription owner receives their share automatically each month, and members pay only for their portion of the subscription.

What if someone stops paying?

We have protection systems in place for subscription owners. If a member stops paying, they'll lose access, and we'll help find a replacement if needed. Your subscription remains secure.

How do you ensure privacy and security?

We never share your personal login credentials with other users. Our platform uses secure access methods that protect your account information while still allowing shared access to the content.
